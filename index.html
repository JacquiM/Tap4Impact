The agricultural communities of South Africa are the backbone of our nation, yet they face unique challenges that demand innovative solutions. The Agri Securitas Trust Fund, operating under the esteemed aegis of Agri SA, was founded in response to these pressing needs, inspired by the Rural Safety Summit held on October 10, 1998.

The Fund is driven by a commitment to build safe and sustainable environments for the people who keep our nation fed. Through pioneering initiatives, we strengthen rural security and, in doing so, protect South Africa‚Äôs food security.

The Project: TAP4IMPACT

TAP4IMPACT is a simple and powerful way for South Africans to give back. By tapping on a digital donation screen, individuals can seamlessly contribute to the protection of farming communities. This initiative boosts the financial resilience of the Trust Fund, ensuring lasting, community-based impact in rural areas.

How It Works

  • Visual Progress: See real-time donation progress on TAP4IMPACT screens.
  • Tap to Donate: Quick and effortless giving.
  • Scan QR Code: View project impact, sponsors, and more.
