Agri Securitas Trust Fund

South Africa’s agricultural communities are the heart of our nation. Yet these communities face mounting complex challenges, particularly concerning safety and sustainability. Inspired by the landmark Rural Safety Summit of October 10, 1998, the Agri Securitas Trust Fund was founded under the valued patronage of Agri SA to confront these challenges with purpose and determination. By funding strategically targeted, innovative projects, the Trust Fund plays a vital role in creating safer rural environments, helping to protect those who feed our nation and ensuring food security for the future.

Funding Of Projects

The Trust Fund’s ability to make a real difference depends on the financial support it receives. While regular fundraising events provide essential funding, long-term success relies on broader participation from individuals, businesses, and communities. To ensure a more stable, sustainable income stream, Agri Securitas, in partnership with Idalia Holdings (Pty) Ltd, has introduced an exciting new initiative.
