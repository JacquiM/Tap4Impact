About TAP4IMPACT

The agricultural communities of South Africa are the backbone of our nation, yet they face unique challenges that demand innovative solutions. The Agri Securitas Trust Fund, operating under the esteemed aegis of Agri SA, was founded in response to these pressing needs, inspired by the Rural Safety Summit held on October 10, 1998.


The Fund is driven by a commitment to build safe and sustainable environments for the people who keep our nation fed. Through pioneering initiatives, we strengthen rural security and, in doing so, protect South Africaâ€™s food security.
